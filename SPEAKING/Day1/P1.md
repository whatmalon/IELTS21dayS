# [Three essential exam questions]
## 1. Studies
### A. Choice & Interesting parts
1. What subjects are you studying? / Are you a student or are you working?
2. Why did you choose to do that subject?
3. Is is very interesting?
### B. Preference
1. who helps you the most? And how?
2. Do you prefer to study in the morning or in the afternoon?
### C. Past & Future
1. What do you plan to do after graduation?
2. Would you like to go to other countries to study?
